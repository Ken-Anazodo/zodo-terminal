Return and Exchange Policy

To be eligible for a return or exchange, products must be returned within 14 days of receiving your order. Please note that all returns and exchanges must be processed through our Returnarea Portal for tracking and processing purposes. Any returns not processed through the portal will unfortunately not be accepted.

For your return or exchange to be accepted, please ensure the following conditions are met:

  • Products must be in their original, unworn condition with all tags attached.
  • Items should be returned in their undamaged original packaging, including the shoebox.
  • The completed Return Form must accompany the returned items.
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable.

If any of these conditions are not met, the return will be rejected and sent back to you. Please ensure the items meet these guidelines to avoid any delays.

Once we receive your return, it will be processed within 10 business days. Please allow up to 5 additional days for the refund to reflect on your account after processing.

If you have any questions regarding your return or exchange, feel free to reach out to us at customerservice@zodoterminal.com.

For any issues with return labels, please contact our partner Returnarea at support@returnarea.com.
