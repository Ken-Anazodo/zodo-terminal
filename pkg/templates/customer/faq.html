Frequently Asked Questions

At ZODO Terminal, we offer a 14-day return policy for all our customers. You can return products in their original packaging and condition within this period, no questions asked.

Shipping usually takes between 3-5 business days for standard delivery. Expedited shipping options are available during checkout for faster delivery.

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. The option is made available while placing your order. Please refer to our shipping policy page for more information.

We accept all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. For large orders, we also accept bank transfers.

Once your order is shipped, we will send you a tracking link via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account on the courier's delivery website.

Upon receiving your delivery, please carefully inspect the package for any signs of damage. If you notice any external damage (e.g., torn packaging, opened box), it is essential to document it immediately in the courier's delivery form, along with your signature, noting the condition of the package. For any other issues or concerns, kindly reach out to our Customer Service team for assistance.

  • Verify that the delivery address provided is accurate.
  • Check your email for any updates or notifications regarding your order.
  • Reach out to the carrier if you missed the delivery or couldn't collect your order.
